# DriveHub  
DriveHub is a curated car listing platform focused on trust, quality, and simplicity. Instead of being an open marketplace where anyone can list vehicles, DriveHub operates as a verified car brokerage platform — ensuring that every listed vehicle meets a certain standard.


## Project Vision
DriveHub aims to become a trusted brand in car sales by:

-Listing only verified vehicles

-Partnering with trusted car dealers (yards)

-Providing a simple and direct buying experience

The goal is not just to display cars, but to build confidence between buyers and sellers.

## Business Model

DriveHub operates on a broker / commission-based model:

-Vehicles are either:
  Personally inspected
  Sourced from trusted partners
-Buyers contact sellers via WhatsApp
-DriveHub earns a commission per successful sale
